01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how FuelMet (Android package com.aswinkl71.fuelmet) handles information. FuelMet is a personal utility that calculates vehicle fuel mileage and estimated trip fuel cost. By installing or using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

02

Who we are

FuelMet is developed and published as an independent Android application. We do not operate user accounts, a cloud backend, or a marketing mailing list for this app.

03

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information. FuelMet does not gather your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, device identifiers for advertising, or payment details.

Numbers you type for calculations (for example distance, fuel used, mileage, and fuel price) are processed only on your device to show results on screen. They are not uploaded to our servers because FuelMet has no such servers for user data.

04

How information is used

Calculation inputs are used solely to compute mileage (distance ÷ fuel) and trip cost ((distance ÷ mileage) × price per litre). We do not use this information for analytics, advertising, profiling, or resale.

05

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties, advertisers, data brokers, or social networks. Because we do not collect personal data from the app, there is nothing to share.

06

On-device storage

FuelMet may keep simple preferences or temporary values using on-device storage (such as Android / Capacitor Preferences). This data remains on your device. It is not transmitted to us. Clearing app data or uninstalling FuelMet removes such local data.

07

Permissions & internet

FuelMet's mileage and cost calculators work fully offline. Fonts and UI assets are bundled with the app — we do not load remote font or analytics scripts.

The Android package may still declare the standard INTERNET permission (common for WebView / Capacitor apps). FuelMet does not send your calculation inputs to a developer-operated server for processing or storage.

08

Third-party services

FuelMet does not integrate advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services that upload personal data, or social login providers.

The Google Play Store itself may collect installation and usage statistics according to Google's own policies when you download apps from Play. That collection is controlled by Google, not by FuelMet's in-app logic.

09

Children's privacy

FuelMet is a general-purpose calculator and does not target children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country).

10

Security

Because calculations happen on your device and we do not operate a user-data cloud for FuelMet, risk of remote data breach of your trip numbers through our systems is minimized. You remain responsible for securing your own device with a lock screen and OS updates.

11

Your choices

You can stop using FuelMet at any time by closing or uninstalling the app. Uninstalling removes the app and its local data from your device (subject to how Android handles backups on your phone).

12

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app's features or legal requirements change. The "last updated" date at the top will change when we revise it. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy. Material changes will be reflected in the app and in the policy URL submitted to Google Play when applicable.
